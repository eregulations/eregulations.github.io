# Public interface

The public interface allows public to access the database through addresses of the following type:</p><p class="lead">http:// "country-or-city name".eregulations.org.

The site is composed of four levels:

### Home page <small>(first level)</small>

<ul>
	<li>A slide-show with welcome messages</li>
	<li>Texts and video messages from the institutions in charge of the project</li>
	<li>Directory of institutions/ministries, forms and legal bases</li>
	<li>Menu of the procedures documented in the system</li>
</ul>

This page can be totally customized through the administrative interface. All texts and photos can be changed.

<h3>Menu page <small>(second level, opens when you click on an objective on the homepage)</small></h3>
<ul>
	<li>Investment sub-objectives</li>
	<li>Explanatory texts</li></ul>
This page can be totally customized through the administrative interface. All texts and photos can be changed.

<h3>Summary page <small>(third level, opens when you click on a procedure/objective)</small></h3>
<ul>
	<li>Left part: list of all steps in the procedure</li>
	<li>Right part: summary of the procedure indicating the total number of institutions involved, showing all results, requirements, costs, duration and legal bases justifying the procedure</li>
</ul>

<h3>Step page <small>(fourth level, when you click on a step)</small></h3>
<ul>
	<li>Left part: list of all steps in the procedure</li>
	<li>Right part: description of the elements for each step</li>
</ul>
The content of this page is automatically generated by the database.

#### Section 1

a. <strong>Logo(s)</strong> of the system  
b. <strong>Logo(s)</strong> of the local counterpart  
c. <strong>Search</strong> tool in the database  

#### Section 2

d. <strong>Home</strong>: takes you to the home page from any part of the system  
e. <strong>Procedures</strong>: shows the complete lits of procedures documented in the system  
f. <strong>About us</strong>: presents the local eRegulations team  
g. <strong>Contact</strong>: visitors can send messages/feedback to the administration.  
h. <strong>Language</strong>: allows a quick translation of the site, powered by google translation   
i. <strong>Slide show</strong>: series of photos with welcoming messages towards the visitor (fully configurable through the administrative interface)   

#### Section 3

j. <strong>Links</strong> to menu pages. This line scrolls if the list of procedures is longer than the size of the frame.  

#### Section 4
k. <strong>Title of the site</strong>  
l. <strong>Texts</strong> presenting the system  

#### Section 5

m.<strong>Directories</strong> of institutions/ministries, forms and legal bases  
n. <strong>Welcome</strong>: link to a video welcome message or to other parts of the system   
o. <strong>How to use the site</strong>: link to a tutorial video or explanation page  
p. <strong>Feedback</strong>: link to a simple form for sending comments, complaints or simplification ideas  


#### Section 6

q. <strong>Title of the objective (procedure)</strong>  
r. <strong>Sub-Menu</strong>  
s. <strong>Links to sub-objectives (procedures)</strong>  

#### Section 7

t. <strong>Logo</strong> of UNCTAD and sponsors/donors (if any)  
u. <strong>Logo(s)</strong> of the local counterpart(s)
